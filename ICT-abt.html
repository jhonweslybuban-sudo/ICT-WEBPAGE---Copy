About Us

We are a team of passionate students from Technological University of the Philippines, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Education major in Information and Communication Technology (BSIE-ICT). With our combined skills in programming, design, content creation, research, and presentation, we have worked together to bring this website to life. Each of us plays a unique role in ensuring that the platform is not only functional and visually appealing but also informative and engaging. From coding interactive elements to designing graphics, writing content, and gathering reliable information, every detail is a result of our teamwork and dedication. We believe that a great website isn’t just about looks—it’s about how well it works and how valuable it is to its users. Through collaboration and shared expertise, we have built a platform that delivers a seamless and meaningful experience. Get to know the talented individuals behind this project and see how we made it possible!

Jhon Wesly T. Buban (WEB PROGRAMMER)

Wesly is the backbone of our website, ensuring everything functions smoothly behind the scenes. He writes the code that makes buttons, links, and pages work properly, creating a seamless user experience. Without his technical expertise, our website would be nothing more than an idea. His work keeps the site fast, responsive, and reliable for all users. Thanks to Wesly, users can navigate our website without any hassle!

Precious Lara Timoteo (WEB CONTENT WRITER)

Lara is the voice of our website, making sure all information is clear, engaging, and easy to understand. She writes everything from team descriptions to project details, ensuring that the users know exactly what our website is about. Her words bring the website to life, guiding users through a smooth and informative experience. A website isn’t just about visuals—it needs strong content, and Lara delivers just that. Without her, our site wouldn’t be as informative, structured, and meaningful as it is.

Charlotte Mae Colon (WEB PRESENTATION DESIGNER)

Charlotte turns information into engaging and well-structured presentations. She designs slideshows and digital materials that make learning easier and more enjoyable. With her keen eye for organization, she ensures that all presentations are clear, effective, and visually appealing. A well-designed presentation can enhance understanding, and Charlotte makes sure every detail is in place. Thanks to her, our content is not only educational but also exciting to look at!

Juliana Marizh Calapputu (WEB CREATIVE DESIGNS)

Juliana is the artist of our team, responsible for making the website look visually appealing. From designing the logo, choosing colors, and creating layouts, she ensures that the website is both beautiful and functional. Her creativity brings everything together, making sure the site is polished and professional. A website should not only work well but also leave a lasting impression, and Juliana makes that possible. Thanks to her artistic touch, our website stands out with eye-catching and well-thought-out designs.

Micaela Lopez (WEB CONTENT CURATOR)

Micaela is our research expert, gathering the best and most accurate information for our website. She ensures that all content is factual, relevant, and valuable to our audience. From instructional materials to background research, she plays a crucial role in making sure our website is well-informed. A website must be built on reliable information, and Micaela works hard to make sure every detail is correct. Her dedication to quality research makes our website both trustworthy and informative.
